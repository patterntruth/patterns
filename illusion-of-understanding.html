Do You Understand — Or Just Recognize?

When asked to explain something you “know,” you usually:

Struggle without notes I know it, but can’t reconstruct it
Explain the surface The logic fades when pressed
Rebuild it clearly I can explain it from first principles

If someone keeps asking “why?”, you feel:

Exposed I hit a wall quickly
Comfortable I can trace the logic deeper
Slightly unsure I can go some distance, then stall

After learning something new, you can usually:

Recognize it later It feels familiar, not usable
Rebuild it alone No reference needed
Recall parts But not the structure
