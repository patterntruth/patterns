You’re not choosing most of your behavior.

1) When you consume content, do you usually leave with something concrete done?

2) Are your days busy, but the main outcome of your life hasn’t changed in months?

3) Do you often “know” what you should change but continue the same behavior anyway?

4) Do decisions in your life usually happen by default rather than by explicit choice?
