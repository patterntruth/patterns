About

Pattern Zero is a collection of behavioral and psychological patterns.

Each pattern is designed to highlight a recurring way people think, decide, or avoid — especially in situations where self-perception and actual behavior quietly diverge.

This site does not diagnose, label, or evaluate people.
It does not provide advice, prescriptions, or solutions.

The patterns here are observational.
They exist to surface blind spots, not to fix them.

If a pattern resonates, that doesn’t mean it is “true.”
If it doesn’t, that doesn’t mean it is wrong.

Patterns are mirrors — not verdicts.

Pattern Zero was built for people who are curious about how they move through the world, not for people looking to be told what to do.
